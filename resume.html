Engineering Futures Mentor @ ASU:

August 2019 - Present //

Engineering Futures is an NSF-funded program that provides social and academic supports to first-year engineering students, especially first-generation college students and minorities in STEM. In this role, I individually met with my 20 assigned mentees each month, planned events with a rotating group, and began a series on iOS development in Swift/Xcode that I'm continuing this semester.

Research Assistant @ ASU:

October 2019 - May 2019 //

This research rests upon the intersection of sustainability and protein quality--a measure of the amino acids in a food and how well humans can absorb them. It makes heavy use of life cycle analysis, which quantifies the environmental impact of a food or product over the span of its existance. My role in this project was researching the protein sources used, gathering the nutritional contents of these foods, and contributing to the algorithm for balancing the sustainability of foods with their protein quality.
