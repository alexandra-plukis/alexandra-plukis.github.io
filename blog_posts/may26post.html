A brief hello.

while my thoughts mature, this'll have to do :-)

I've always had a "blog" tab on every iteration of this website, but it's never been populated with a single post. In the past I've wanted to perfect the styling of this site before starting on any real posts. Looking back, that feels like an obvious concession to perfectionism! But now with my mild redesign, that pressure is off. Can't wait for perfect UI if you accept that the "perfect" UI is always changing :think-smart:
Frankly, I feel a sheepish having a blog section at all. My thoughts don't feel mature enough; I don't have much experience in my field; my opinions change on professional work on a weekly basis; I don't have enough side projects; I haven't written long form essays in literal years... basically, I feel a little silly!
So, while you see that this section of my website is titled "blog," please know that I think of it much more like a journal. My purpose here is to jot down some thoughts as I explore the wonderful and vast field of software development. I'm hoping this will be useful to myself in the following ways:
  1. Documenting the things I'm learning about.
  2. Posting some processes I'm fiddling with in my programming practice.
  3. Having fun with the written word!
If it's useful to you too, dear reader, then that is the cherry on top!
That's all for now. See you next time!
